About Girls Who Code SJ

The Girls Who Code San Jose Club is a club established in 2014 through the national Girls Who Code Clubs Program. Volunteer teachers (working professionals with a career in technology, or Lynbrook High School students) teach computer science to girls in grades 6 through 8 at Miller Middle School. Subjects that are (or have been) taught include Javascript, HTML and CSS, Python, and SAM Labs. For more on Girls Who Code Clubs, visit: http://girlswhocode.com/clubs/ For more about our specific club, contact us at: girlswhocodesj@gmail.com
